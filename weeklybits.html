Weekly Puzzles


Every week, I will release three smaller crossword puzzles. Try and complete them as quick as you can!

Bit puzzles are 5x5 and the easiest puzzles of the set. Nibble puzzles are 7x7 and are a bit harder. Bite puzzles are 9x9 and are the hardest of the bunch.
