Update 12/1/25

Happy December!

Shorter blog post today, but you're still getting the same usual releases here.

Starting off. I have three new weekly puzzles! With these ones, I experimented with including more 2-letter words to see if it makes the puzzles any more or less interesting. You'll have to let me know what you think :)

Then, I have another Friday-difficulty puzzle to put on the site! I think this is the second time that I've had to edit a clue in a puzzle because it became outdated in the months it sat in my drafts. Unfortunately, there are probably many more errors that I haven't caught in puzzles already on the site. I think this is a sign that I both need to really proofread my puzzles before releasing them AND that I just need to get through this backlog faster.

One last thing I'll mention is that I created a Reddit account to try and promote my puzzles on there. In the off chance you're from Reddit and reading this: Hello! I hope you like the puzzles.

Happy solving everyone!

- Buck
