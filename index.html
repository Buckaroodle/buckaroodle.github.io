Update 2/23/26

Hello all! I hope you're having a wonderful week so far. I'm excited to get into this week's puzzles, so let's do that!

Week 24 of weekly puzzles have arrived! Best of luck to you all with them!

Now, I'd like to give some context for this week's puzzle drop here. About a month or so ago, I finished all of the puzzles in Francis Heaney's "New Grids on the Block" crossword book. It was a brilliantly put-together collection of puzzles filled with just so many 90s references and homages to things that I have zero nostalgia for being a Gen-Zer. Despite this, the puzzles were amazing, and after finishing that book, I was inspired to make a 90s-themed puzzle of my own, and that's what this week's puzzle is! "Big Band" is a Wednesday-difficulty puzzle that I tried to stuff as much 90s stuff into as I possibly could! Even if you're a Gen-Zer like me, I still think you'll have a fun time with it.

Good luck guys, and happy solving!

- Buck
