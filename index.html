Update 12/29/25

Here we are. The last website update of 2025! And thus, the last crossword drop of the year!

Firstly, a quick shoutout to my sister for catching a small typo in my Week 15 Bite puzzle which will be accompanied by an apology to Carly Rae Jepsen and not Carly Rae Jepson. Remember that if you spot a typo or error in one of my puzzles, feel free to contact me either through email or Discord. And for those who know me personally, you can even call me ... maybe :)

My last 3 weekly puzzles of the year are here and ready for you to solve! Best of luck everyone!

Lastly, I have a Monday-difficulty puzzle ready for solving too. It's got a very simple but subtle theme that, if you can figure it out, will definitely aid you in figuring out the theme entries.

I don't wanna make my blog post too long here, but I do really want to give a thank you to anybody reading this post right now. This website and all the puzzles have been such a passion project of mine for the past couple years, and it truly means so much to me that anybody plays my puzzles in the first place.

Thank you all, and I'll see you in 2026! Happy solving!

- Buck
