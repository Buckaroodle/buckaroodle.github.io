Update 4/13/26

Hello everyone! Apologies for missing last week's update. It's been a wild week for me, but I'm excited to release this week's puzzles!

Week 30 of weekly puzzles are here! If there was ever a set of puzzles to play to practice your crossword-ese, then these are those puzzles! Writing unique clues for three-letter entries is an often tedious and difficult process, and so I didn't do that at all. Generic clues incoming! Good luck!

As I stated last update, I do not a big puzzle to release this week. However, if you're interested in a bonus puzzle, then look no further! Provided here is a PDF of a variety puzzle I constructed recently! Variety puzzles are often similar to crossword puzzles but rely on a unique set of rules. Unfortunately, because of this, I can't upload this puzzle to squares.io and can only distribute it via PDF. If you're interested in trying it out, I'd recommend printing it out or screenshotting it with your phone or tablet and using any drawing app you like. Good luck to all who take on the challenge! (feel free to contact me if you want an answer key!)

I've got less than a month before I graduate college which is both exciting and also making me extremely nervous. I hope I can continue to find the time to create cool puzzles for you all in the future.

Happy solving everyone!

- Buck
