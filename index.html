Update 7/29/25

Huge news!

I am so excited to finally announce that I am now the proud owner of my own web domain! Even if my website changes hosts, you'll always be able to find it with buckscrosswords.xyz! I honestly wish I had done this months earlier. Huzzah!

Anyways, new crosswords! My dad told me he tried one of the Friday puzzles I had uploaded last time and said he was very lost which makes sense cause they were all Gen-Z-pilled. Don't worry Dad, I've got some more approachable puzzles right here!. School Slice is a more beginner-friendly puzzle, however I gave it a Tuesday difficulty rating instead of a Monday rating because of a few particularly tricky and deceptive clues [Tech company that sticks in your brain? (9)]. Alphabetical Order has a simple theme with some really silly clues that make me chuckle [Diet that, according to my research, does not require eating dinosaurs (5)]. Lastly, Bollards is another pop culture-filled themeless puzzle containing references that my dad will never in a million years understand [Bunny-like character from YouTube's "The Amazing Digital Circus" (3)].

I hope you all find the new domain as cool and exciting as I do, and hey, maybe I'll get a fun logo in the near future lol. Happy solving!

- Buck
