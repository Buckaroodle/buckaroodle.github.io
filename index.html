Update 9/8/25

Hello everyone! I'm back with another set of weekly puzzles and a standard-sized crossword too!

The Week 2 Bit, Nibble, and Bite puzzles are up! As a reminder, these puzzles are smaller than my normal puzzles at 5x5, 7x7, and 9x9 squares respectively, but this doesn't mean that they're easier than my normal puzzles! The Bit is the easiest, while the Bite is much tougher. Try and complete them as fast as you can! (BTW if you want to see your timer, click the settings icon in the blue bar in the puzzle window and enable "Show Timer"). If you missed out on last week's puzzles, they are still available to play with links to them underneath the current week's puzzles.

Today's standard-sized puzzle is a really fun one! Most of the time, my crosswords have the standard rotational symmetry which can make putting in the theme words tough. However, if I think the theme is fun enough like this one, I'll switch to left-right symmetry which allows me a bit more freedom in terms of theme word length and placement. I won't spoil too much else about the puzzle, you'll just have to trust me on this one :)

Good luck everyone and happy solving!

- Buck
