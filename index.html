Update 11/17/25

Hello again!

I'm back from my brief hiatus with a new batch of puzzles for you all! As usual, I've got 3 new weekly puzzles and a full-sized Friday puzzle from the backlog!

The weekly puzzles are a bit trickier than normal this week but not in an unfair way I don't think. However, I also think the clues this week are more fun and exciting. Good luck with them!

Honestly, the same thing could be said about the Friday puzzle this week. Quite tricky with some rather silly clues (Fee-fi-___-fum (possible name for a nursery rhyme-themed Asian restaurant?) (3)). Definitely a puzzle I'm proud of!

As next week is right around Thanksgiving time, I'll have a Thanksgiving puzzle finished and ready for upload. As much as I've been enjoying these pop-culture themeless puzzles, I do want to have some themed puzzles too to give some more variety to the new uploads.

As always, thanks for playing my crosswords everyone! Happy solving!
