Update 12/22/25

Happy last day of Hanukkah! Also, I'm sick!

Well, I was sick for a good bit of this week. Spent a good amount of time sitting in bed coughing and sneezing. However, it gave me a good amount of time to not only play some crossword puzzles, but work on some of my own!

I've got the three new weekly puzzles ready for solving! I found the Bite to be particularly difficult to satisfyingly fill this week, so I apologize in advance if that one feels a little half-baked.

If you'd like a full-sized crossword that's on the easier side while still having some interesting clues, "Barely Bare" is a Tuesday-difficulty release with a simple theme that includes some unique grid entries. Those grid entries are the reason it's a Tuesday rather than a Monday.

I'm already feeling much better than I was yesterday, so I hope to be fully recovered by either tomorrow or the next day. Only time will tell, I suppose. Good luck with the puzzles everyone!

- Buck
